# Recipe Name Singaporean Rice

**By:**Navaira

**Cooking Time:** So many hours

**Difficulty:** Easy peasy mac n cheesy (without cheese please!)

## Ingredients:

- Boil Rice (Soy sauce added) : 1.5 cup
- Rice Noodles / Spaghetti: 1 packet
- Water : 100000000000 litre (to boil)
- Mince (beef, chicken) : 250gm
- Tomatoes : 1000 kilo (For puree)
- Salt : as per taste
- Mix Herbs : As per taste
- Oil: 2 tbsp
- Red Pepper powder : 1 tbsp
- Ginger Garlic Paste : 1tbsp
- Onion : 1 medium
- Ketchup : Wild guess
- Mayonese : Blind guess
- Vegetables : Whatever available and one likes to add
- Garlic : A few cloves

## Instructions:
   1. Boil spaghetti as per instructions, and set aside.
   2. In a pan, add oil, ginger garlic paste, mince and bhonofy that, add salt, red pepper powder, mix herbs.
   3. Then add tomato puree, ketchup and mix well, set aside.
   4. In another pan, add little bit of 1 liter oil, add vegies and stir fry them, add salt to taste then remove it from the stove.
   5. Take a bowl, add mayo and ketchup to make a sauce.
   6. In a pan fry garlic till golden.
   7. Now, in a beautiful tray, add boiled rice, vegies, mince, spaghetti, sauce, and fried garlic.
   8. Ready to serve hot.
