# DINNER RECIPE

---

### Ranum’s “Vecna’s Midnight Macaroni Madness”


** By: Ranum*

*Cooking Time: Until the clock chimes four times*

**Difficulty: Cursed*

---

**Ingredients:*

1. Macaroni

2. Cheese (unholy amount)

3. Chili flakes

4. One emotional breakdown

5. A spoon (to fight Vecna if needed)

---

**Instructions:*

1. Boil the pasta like you’re boiling all your problems.

2. Add cheese until the ancestors start watching you.

3. Mix chili flakes to summon flavor demons.

4. Serve in a bowl that definitely isn’t haunted.

---

**Why This Recipe?*

Every night, when hunger strikes and sanity leaves, I make this.
Vecna can take my soul, but NOT my macaroni.
